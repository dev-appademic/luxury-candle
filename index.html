Our Story

A home is built from small rituals.

Founded in a small studio in the Hudson Valley, Candle Company was born from a single obsession: that a candle should never be ordinary. Not in the scent. Not in the burn. Not in the vessel it leaves behind.

We partner with master perfumers from Grasse and source only the rarest naturals — Mysore sandalwood, Calabrian bergamot, Omani frankincense. Each fragrance is developed over months, poured by hand, and finished in glass we design ourselves.

Why Candle Company

Made slowly, so you notice.

Clean Composition

Coconut-soy wax, lead-free cotton wicks, and phthalate-free fragrance oils. Nothing you'd hesitate to breathe.

Rare Naturals

Sourced from Grasse, Mysore, and Calabria. We don't approximate — we import the source material itself.

Slow Pour

Every candle cures for seven days before it leaves our studio. Patience is the difference between a scent and a feeling.

Second Life

Our vessels are designed to live on — as tumblers, planters, or quiet keepers. Refill kits available.
